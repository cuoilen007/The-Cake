The Cake Story__________

The Cake Shop was founded by a team of passionate and dedicated bakers who are committed in baking the most delicious cakes and pastries around. We have evolved to become one of a premium distributor and wholesaler for cakes and pastries to some well known resturants, cafes, supermart, hotels and bakery.
